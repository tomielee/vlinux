        s/              >> start sed replace
        .*              >> everything from the beginning
        ([0-9]{2})      >> group 1 = digits, two (dd)
        +\/             >> plus, "/" with the escape \ 
        ([A-Za-z]{3})   >> group 2 = letters, 3 (Mmm)
        ([0-9]{4})      >> group 3 = digits, 4 (yyyy)
        .*              >> everything after

        /               >> replace with...

        nothing         >> .* is replaced with nothing
        \3              >> group 3
        \-              >> - with escape \
        ... same with 2 and 1
        nothing         >> .* is replaced with nothing

        /               >> end of se replace
